BD250 Pipeline Process Map

Active pursuit playbook ‚Äî signal-driven outreach for in-market agencies
What Is BD250
A curated list of ~250 agencies showing active buying signals ‚Äî RFP alerts, budget line items, GovSpend triggers, board minutes mentions, vendor complaints. These agencies have been researched and qualified before they hit this list. The pipeline tracks active pursuit of high-value opportunities.
How It's Different From GTM
GTM pipeline nurtures ICP leads who aren't actively buying yet. BD250 pursues agencies that are in-market now. Outreach references specific buying signals. Cadence is faster. The goal is to build a relationship before the RFP drops ‚Äî so when procurement goes live, you're already the agency they've been talking to.
Three Outcomes
Won Direct: They buy through a co-op contract (Sourcewell, NASPO, OMNIA) or sole source ‚Äî no RFP needed.
Moved to RFP: Formal procurement goes live ‚Äî deal moves to the RFP pipeline where you're already positioned.
Lost: Went cold or chose a competitor ‚Äî re-engage in 6 months.

This Pipeline ‚Äî GTM Prospecting
ICP Leads from Sales Navigator
These leads match our ideal customer profile but may not be actively looking yet. Sourced from 6 saved searches in Sales Navigator. The goal is to build relationships through value-first outreach, watch for buying signals, and find a small entry point to get inside the door.
Land & Expand Strategy: Don't pitch the big engagement. Listen for a small pain point and offer a starter project ‚Äî AI Readiness Assessment ($15-30K), Technology Strategy Review ($20-50K), Data Governance Assessment ($15-25K), or ERP Health Check ($10-20K). Once inside doing good work, the big engagement follows.
Separate Track ‚Äî BD250
Active Pursuit List
These are agencies we know are in the market ‚Äî they've issued an RFP, entered a budget cycle, or publicly signaled a need. These get a more direct sales approach. The BD250 list has its own process and is not managed through this GTM pipeline.
Key difference: GTM prospects need nurturing and relationship-building before a sale. BD250 agencies are ready for a direct value proposition and proposal.
